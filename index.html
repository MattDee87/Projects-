30 Min Tomato Sauce

I welcome you to enjoy a quick and easy tomato sauce brought down from my family to yours. This was my great great grandmothers recipe that she made in her resturant in Saratoga Springs New York. We call this our 30 min sauce and I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

One can of whole San Marzano Tomatos

Two Cloves of Garlic

One TSP Red Pepper Flakes

Two TBSP of Extra Vergin Olive Oil (EVOO)

One TBSP of dried or fresh Parsley

One TBSP of dried or fresh Basil

Salt and Pepper to taste


Directions

  1. Pour two TBSP of EVOO in a large non-stick cooking pan.
  2. Stir in one TSP of Red Pepper Flakes and let stand for 1 min. Then add chopped garlic until browned and aromatic.
  3. Slowly pour in one Can Whole Tomatos. Next, use potato masher and mash-mash-mash! Be carful of splatter *see tips below*.
  1. Let sit until everything comes to a boil.
  2. Add dried or fresh Parsley, Basil and a pinch of Salt and Pepper to taste. Simmer for 20 min stirring occasionally.
  3. Serve with your favorite pasta and enjoy!


  • Use knife to slice tomatos. This makes sure the juice inside doesn't splatter everywhere when mashing!
  • Add a sprinkle of Parmesan Cheese
  • Garnish with fresh Basil leafs
